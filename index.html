Meet Robert

Robert Raleigh is an active entrepreneur with 10 years of experience in web development, mobile develpoment, parternship development, and fundraising. Robert is the President, and Founder of Log Camp. He is an alumni, and mentor at the Portland Incubator Experiment (PIE).

Robert is an Alaskan-Native (Tlingit), and a former foster-child. He is also a member of the Oregon Entrepreneurs Network (OEN), The Indus Entrepreneurs (TIE), Technology Association of Oregon (TAO), and the Portland City Club. Robert lives in Portland, OR.
